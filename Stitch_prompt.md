# Stitch UI Prompt — Jane Wei Personal Website

## Overview
Design a personal homepage for Jane Wei. The core purpose is to showcase personal work/projects and provide a way to contact her.

---

## Pages

### 1. Home Page
- Display a logo made of the letters **"JW"** (both uppercase) as the primary brand mark in the top area
- Main hero section contains a bold welcome headline, e.g. **"Welcome to Jane Wei's Homepage"**
- Below the hero text, place a prominent **"Contact Me"** call-to-action button

### 2. About Me Page
- Two distinct sections:
  - **Good Part** — a personal highlight section (content TBD)
  - **Bad Part** — a candid/honest personal reflection section (content TBD)

### 3. Contact Me Page
- Display WeChat ID
- Display email address
- Keep layout clean and minimal

### 4. My Agents Page
- Showcase a small collection of AI agents related to Jane Wei's life and work (names/content TBD)
- Present each agent as a card or labeled block

---

## Navigation
- **Position:** Fixed **left-side vertical sidebar**
- **Nav items:** Home Page · About Me · Contact Me · My Agents
- **Behavior:** Click to navigate between pages (single-page app style)

---

## Visual Style
- **Aesthetic:** Industrial design — clean, bold, mechanical precision (inspired by brands like BMW, Tesla)
- Emphasize strong geometry, sharp edges, and purposeful whitespace
- Minimal ornamentation; let structure and typography carry the design

---

## Color Scheme
| Role | Color |
|---|---|
| Primary | Black `#000000` |
| Primary | White `#FFFFFF` |
| Primary | Royal Blue `#002366` |
| Accent | Yellow `#FFD700` |
| Text highlight | Neon purple gradient (e.g. `#BF5FFF` → `#7B2FFF`) |

---

## Interaction & Animation
| Trigger | Effect |
|---|---|
| Page transition | Fade in / Fade out |
| Scroll | No scroll animation |
| Mouse hover (on interactive elements) | Small fish blowing bubbles animation |
| Page loading | Animated cartoon running figure (sprinting/racing effect) |

---

## Notes
- The overall tone should feel modern, confident, and slightly edgy — not generic or corporate
- Typography should be strong and geometric (consider bold sans-serif fonts)
- Mobile responsiveness is a bonus but desktop-first is the priority
